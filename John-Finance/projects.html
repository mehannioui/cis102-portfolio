My Projects

Stock Market Analysis

In this project, I conducted an in-depth analysis of the technology sector in the S&P 500. Using Python and financial APIs, I gathered historical data on stock prices, earnings reports, and market trends. I then applied various statistical methods to identify patterns and potential investment opportunities.

Key findings included a correlation between certain tech companies' R&D spending and their stock performance, as well as the impact of global events on the sector's volatility. This project enhanced my skills in data analysis, Python programming, and financial modeling.

Cryptocurrency Investment Strategy

For this project, I developed a comprehensive investment strategy for cryptocurrency portfolios. I researched the top 20 cryptocurrencies by market cap, analyzing their historical performance, technology fundamentals, and adoption rates.

Using Excel, I created a risk assessment model that factored in market volatility, regulatory risks, and technological advancements. The final product was a dynamic portfolio allocation tool that adjusts investment recommendations based on an investor's risk tolerance and market conditions. This project deepened my understanding of alternative investments and risk management in the digital asset space.

Tech Startup Valuation

In this capstone project, I performed a comprehensive valuation of a high-growth tech startup. Using financial statements and market data, I applied various valuation methods including Discounted Cash Flow (DCF), Comparable Company Analysis, and Precedent Transactions Analysis.

I created detailed financial models in Excel, forecasting the company's growth trajectory and potential market share. The project culminated in a presentation to a panel of professors and industry professionals, where I defended my valuation and recommendations. This experience honed my financial modeling skills and deepened my understanding of how investors value early-stage companies in the tech sector.
