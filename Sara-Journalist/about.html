About Me

Hello! I'm Sarah Johnson, a passionate journalist-in-training at UC Berkeley. My journey into the world of journalism began with a simple curiosity about the world around me and a desire to share important stories with others.

Currently in my junior year, I'm majoring in Media Studies with a minor in Political Science. This interdisciplinary approach allows me to explore the intersections of media, politics, and society, giving me a unique perspective on current events and social issues.

My Journey

My passion for journalism was ignited during my high school years when I joined the school newspaper. Since then, I've been constantly seeking opportunities to hone my craft. At UC Berkeley, I've been an active member of The Daily Californian, where I've covered everything from campus events to local politics.

Skills and Interests

  • Investigative Reporting
  • Feature Writing
  • Multimedia Storytelling
  • Data Journalism
  • Podcast Production

Extracurricular Activities

Outside of my academic pursuits, I'm deeply involved in campus life. I serve as the Vice President of the Berkeley Journalism Association, where I organize workshops and networking events for aspiring journalists. I'm also a volunteer at Berkeley's community radio station, KALX, where I've had the opportunity to produce and host my own show focused on local issues and student life.

When I'm not chasing stories or editing articles, you can find me exploring the diverse neighborhoods of the Bay Area, camera in hand, always on the lookout for untold stories and unique perspectives.
