Welcome to My Portfolio

As an aspiring journalist and media professional, I'm passionate about uncovering truth and sharing impactful stories. My journey in journalism began at the University of California, Berkeley, where I'm currently pursuing a degree in Media Studies with a minor in Political Science.

Through my coursework and extracurricular activities, I've developed a keen eye for detail, strong research skills, and the ability to craft compelling narratives across various media platforms. From investigative reporting to feature writing, I strive to bring depth and nuance to every story I tell.

On this site, you'll find examples of my work, including articles I've written for our campus newspaper, multimedia projects, and my experiences as an intern at local news stations. I'm excited to share my journey with you and showcase how I'm working towards making a difference in the world of journalism.
