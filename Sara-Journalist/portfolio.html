My Portfolio

Here are some of my most significant projects and articles that showcase my skills and passion for journalism.

Campus Sustainability Initiative

In this investigative piece, I delved into UC Berkeley's efforts to become a zero-waste campus by 2025. Through interviews with administration officials, environmental experts, and student activists, I explored the challenges and successes of this ambitious initiative. The article, published in The Daily Californian, sparked important conversations about sustainability practices on campus and led to increased student involvement in green initiatives.

My research involved analyzing years of waste management data, attending city council meetings, and even participating in a campus-wide waste audit. This project not only honed my data journalism skills but also deepened my understanding of environmental reporting and its impact on local communities.

Voices of the Pandemic

During the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, I conceptualized and produced a podcast series titled "Voices of the Pandemic." This series featured intimate conversations with Berkeley residents from all walks of life, documenting their experiences, challenges, and moments of resilience during this unprecedented time.

Over the course of ten episodes, I interviewed healthcare workers, small business owners, teachers, and students, weaving their stories into a powerful narrative about community strength and adaptability. The podcast not only provided a platform for unheard voices but also served as a historical record of our community's response to the global crisis. This project significantly improved my audio editing skills and taught me the art of storytelling through sound.
