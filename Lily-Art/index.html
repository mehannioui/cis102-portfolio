About Me

Hello! I'm Lily Chen, a digital artist and illustrator based in New York City. My work explores the intersection of technology and traditional art forms, blending digital techniques with classic artistic principles.

As a junior at the New York School of Visual Arts, I'm constantly pushing the boundaries of my creativity and technical skills. When I'm not in class or working on projects, you can find me sketching in Central Park or exploring the city's vibrant art scene.

My passion for art extends beyond the digital realm. I'm an active member of the school's Art and Technology Club, where we experiment with cutting-edge tools and techniques. I also volunteer at a local community center, teaching basic digital art skills to underprivileged youth.
