Neon Nights

Neon Nights is a series of digital illustrations inspired by the vibrant nightlife of Tokyo and the cyberpunk aesthetic. Each piece in this series explores the interplay of light and shadow in urban environments, with a focus on the way artificial light shapes our perception of the city after dark.

I used a combination of vector graphics and digital painting to create these pieces, experimenting with various brush techniques to capture the glow and reflections of neon lights. This project allowed me to delve into color theory and composition, creating dynamic and eye-catching scenes that draw the viewer into the bustling energy of the city.

Nature's Palette

Nature's Palette is an ongoing project that combines my love for the natural world with digital art techniques. Each piece in this series starts with a photograph I've taken during hikes or nature walks, which I then transform into a stylized digital painting.

The goal of this project is to capture the essence and emotion of a scene rather than creating a photorealistic reproduction. I use a limited color palette for each piece, challenging myself to convey the complexity of nature through a simplified color scheme. This project has helped me develop my skills in color theory, composition, and digital brushwork.
