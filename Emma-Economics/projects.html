My Projects

Economic Impact Analysis of Remote Work

In this project, I conducted a comprehensive analysis of the economic impacts of the shift to remote work during and after the COVID-19 pandemic. Using data from various sources including the Bureau of Labor Statistics and company surveys, I examined changes in productivity, real estate markets, and urban economics.

Key findings included a significant reduction in commercial real estate demand in urban centers, increased productivity in certain sectors, and a shift in consumer spending patterns. I used R for data analysis and visualization, creating compelling charts to illustrate these trends. This project enhanced my skills in data analysis, economic modeling, and policy evaluation.

Econometric Analysis of Minimum Wage Effects

For this project, I performed an econometric analysis to study the effects of minimum wage increases on employment and income distribution. I collected panel data from 50 U.S. states over a 20-year period, controlling for various economic factors such as GDP growth, inflation, and education levels.

Using STATA, I applied fixed-effects regression models and difference-in-differences analysis. The results suggested that moderate minimum wage increases had minimal negative effects on employment while significantly reducing income inequality. This project deepened my understanding of labor economics and enhanced my skills in econometric analysis and statistical software use.

Economic Feasibility of Renewable Energy Transition

In this capstone project, I evaluated the economic feasibility of a rapid transition to renewable energy sources in a mid-sized European country. I developed a comprehensive economic model that incorporated factors such as infrastructure costs, job market shifts, energy price projections, and environmental externalities.

Using Python, I created a Monte Carlo simulation to account for uncertainties in technological progress and policy changes. The analysis revealed that while the initial costs were significant, the long-term economic benefits outweighed them, especially when factoring in reduced healthcare costs and climate change mitigation. I presented these findings to a panel of professors and industry experts, defending my methodology and conclusions. This project showcased my ability to tackle complex economic problems, use advanced modeling techniques, and present findings effectively.
